One more trick to win the Aviator plane game is the d’Alembert Strategy. This method is based on the theory of the famous 18th-century French philosopher and mathematician Jean Leron d’Alembert.

3:1 Strategy. It is considered one of the most successful strategies for mastering the Aviator slot. By it, the player must always make two bets and always 3:1. For example, if you made one bet of $10, you should place the second one of $30. The goal of this strategy is for the larger bet to cover the smaller one. A lower bet is designed to receive all the profits, assuming zero risk.

When you reach the x1.5 multiplier, you cash out the larger bet and get $30. This will cover the bet amount and bring you to zero if the second bet suddenly loses. If you reach x5 with the second bet, your income will be $80, and your net profit will be $50.

1.5x +3x Strategy. Everything here is similar to how it happens in a single bet strategy. We make the first bet larger, for example, $10 on odds of 1.5. In this case, it might even be better to use the auto bet and auto cashout to focus on the second bet. We make the second bet manually with a x3 multiplier of $5.
