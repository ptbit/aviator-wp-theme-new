Labouchère System

Also known as cancellation strategy or split Martingale, Labouchère revolves around winning up to a certain amount.

Before betting, you decide how much you intend to make at the end of the game sessions. Then, divide it into smaller sums according to your preference. Afterward, add the first and last amounts and wager on one round in Aviator.

If it wins, you cross them off your list, but you add that amount to the bottom if it's a loss.

Here's a better illustration of how this approach to the game works: Assuming you aim to get 2200, you can split it into 215, 320, 320, 330. 340, 330, 320, 215, and R10.

Pros: It can help recoup losses easily. Cons: There's a high risk of losing all bet amounts.
