Gambling Legality in India

The main legislation regulating gambling in India is the Public Gambling Act of 1867, which prohibits running and participating in land-based gambling activities. However, this does not include any provisions covering online gambling, since the law was enacted before the digital era.

As a result, India has not enacted any federal law that absolutely prohibits or restricts online gambling. The effect has been the creation of a legal «gray area» where online gambling is neither prohibited nor fully legalized under federal law.
